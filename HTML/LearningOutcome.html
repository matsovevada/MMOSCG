    • Learning Outcome

    The purpose of Qara is to improve children's skills in regards to literacy and reading ability. Children will be solving puzzles relating to language, vocabulary and text comprehension. In order to raise these skills, we are making a game which will be relaxing, educational and entertaining.


    As mentioned in the "Plot and characters page", the setting of this game is to help a village with learning to read. So an example of gameplay will be "Word Search", in which the child will be tasked to find several words relating to a situation, or a topic. E.G. the town baker in the game world will need help to restore a recipe, and the player will have to find ingredient words within a jumbled mess of letters.

    This is an example of how the task could start

    This is an example of a "Word Search" game. This challenges the players to find words they have learned in earlier tasks.

    Another example of a task is "Connect the word", where the player will be given a list of words, and has to connect matching words, opposite words, any kind of pairing of words that might be relevant to what the player is supposed to learn from the task.

    As the effects of living under long term stress can lead to learning disabilities and impede brain development, we believe it important to help the children relax, and have fun, while also developing reading and writing ability. The game aims to encourage children to learn through challenges and rewards, engaging tasks and fun and interactive gameplay.
