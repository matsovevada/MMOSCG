Join our village

The villagers of Qara are in need of help. They struggle with literacy and the player needsto help them through a wide range of challenges. Literacy is a crucial part of everyday life and its integral for personal development. The game aims to both highlight the importance of literacy and develop the skills of the player.

As with most things in life, the experience is better when shared. The player will meet different characters in game and is tasked with helping them with their issues. With focus on the empowerment of others the player is challenged to solve increasingly more difficult tasks using their literacy skills.
