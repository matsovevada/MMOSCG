    FAQ

    Q: Why is the company called StudApp?

    A: StudApp is an abbreviation for Study Application, and was chosen because the company's specialty is making educational apps.

    Q: My company needs an app developed. Can you do this for us?

    A: Yes! Describe the app you need developed using the contact form below and we will contact you.

    Q: What kind of apps are you able to develop?

    A: StudApp can develop apps for all mobile operating systems. We can make apps with a wide range of functionality suitable for your needs, tough our specialty lies in educational apps.

    Q: What is the cost of having an app developed?

    A: The cost of having an app developed depends on several factors, such as the complexity of the app and the time frame of the project. The average cost of an app development project is about 40 000 US dollars.

    Q: How can I get a job in StudApp?

    A: Currently we are not looking to hire more people, but feel free to send us an open application using the contact form below.
