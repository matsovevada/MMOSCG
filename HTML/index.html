Welcome to Qara

Qara is a new and exciting learning game for iOS and Android. The app aims to serve as a learning supplement for Syrian children. In the village of Qara the player is tasked with helping the villagers using their reading and writing skills. The tasks get increasingly more difficult as the player develops their literacy along with the progression of the game.

Our main focus in the development of Qara is giving the player a stress-free environment where they can develop their skills in their own tempo. The tasks are focused around empathy and are mainly non-competitive.

The player is guided by “Lizzie” - our very own literate lizard! Lizzie is equipped with “the Kitab”, a book that keeps track of previous learning. If the player is stuck on a task they can consult Lizzie by either looking at previous challenges or ask for hints. Lizzie will keep track of the players development and offer encouragement when needed.
